Our work is inspired by the needs of the youths and the continent. We focus majorly on the achievement of the Agenda 2063 and SDGs 4, 8, 16 and 17 of the United Nations and we do this by exposing our participants to the wealth of experience of industry leaders in three key aspects (Leadership, business and digital transformation). Furthermore, we supervise projects and community interventions to help us achieve the stipulated goals.
Since, youths are the upcoming leaders of the nation we find it necessary to hold leadership, enterpreneurial and social impact projects.


DMC Programs

Securing your future
Securing your future (SYF) is a program aimed at inspiring, training and equipping the GEN Z and millenials with tech skills and quality information that makes them valuable to any organization and position them strategically for employment opportunities. It's aimed at establishing fresh graduates and other young people in a particular profession.
Ogun Business Summit
Ogun Business Summit (OBS) is aimed at inspiring and empowering small business owners in Ogun State with quality information on how to launch, manage and build global businesses using realities of the Nigerian marketplace and navigating the challenges that comes with entrepreneurship.
This program would stipulate the creation of more businesses across Ogun state, but not just ordinary businesses but businesses build on the solid foundation (informed decisions), it would create a competitive atmosphere which is necessary for economic growth and ultimately create abundance employment opportunities for the teeming unemployed population.
Community Development Programs
We embark on specific projects in the year to support the community indigenes as part of our corporate social responsibility. Most of the projects will be based on environmental hygiene, empowerment programs and vocational trainings.
University Graduates Conference
This is an annual conference held in several universities across the country and continent as a whole which is focused on enlightening graduates with career tips, job opportunities, post-degree scholarships and empowering them with in-demand tech skills.
